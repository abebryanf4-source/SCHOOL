About Our School

Rock View Nursery and Primary School is dedicated to providing quality education and a nurturing environment for young learners. Our experienced faculty focuses on developing critical thinking, creativity, and character in every student.

We believe in a holistic approach to education, combining academic excellence with extracurricular activities to foster well-rounded development.

Our Programs

Nursery

Ages 2-4: Early childhood development with play-based learning and basic literacy skills.

Kindergarten

Ages 4-5: Foundation for primary education with emphasis on social and emotional development.

Primary (Grades 1-6)

Ages 6-12: Comprehensive curriculum covering Mathematics, English, Science, and Social Studies.
